ELEVATE YOUR NIGHT OUT

Canopy Lounge by Tigerbay is Kuala Lumpur’s rooftop escape high above the city lights. Perched in the heart of KL, our lounge brings together premium shisha, crafted cocktails and skyline views for an experience you’ll remember long after the night ends. Step out onto our open-air terrace or sink into a plush corner in the VIP lounge while our team takes care of everything. From exclusive shisha blends sourced from around the world to signature drinks mixed at the bar, every detail is designed for you to unwind, celebrate and feel on top of the city.

Lush greenery, warm lighting and vibrant accents frame uninterrupted views of the Petronas Towers, creating an atmosphere that’s both energetic and intimate. From sunset hangouts and late-night drinks to live sports and themed events, Canopy Lounge is where every evening takes on a life of its own.
